Track, organize, and enhance your gaming experience with GameLog, the ultimate app for managing your video game collection and playtime. GameLog is the ultimate companion for every gamer, empowering you to effortlessly organize your games, explore exciting new titles, and stay up-to-date with the latest releases. With GameLog, tracking your playthroughs, progress, and playtime has never been easier. Plus, you can share your gaming adventures with friends and followers on social media, showcasing your achievements and experiences. Features: Organize and Streamline: Effortlessly manage your game collection by creating personalized lists with intuitive filters and sorting options. Stay in control as you curate and categorize your games exactly the way you want. Track Your Gaming Journey: Log and track your playthroughs, progress, and playtime for each game. Gain valuable insights into your gaming habits and milestones, helping you better understand and appreciate your gaming journey. Discover New Gaming Horizons: Immerse yourself in the world of gaming discovery with our powerful game finder and extensive video game database. Explore an ever-growing collection of titles, uncover hidden gems, and broaden your gaming repertoire. Stay Ahead of the Game: Never miss a beat with GameLog's release date countdowns, keeping you informed about upcoming releases and ensuring you're always prepared to dive into the latest gaming experiences. Share Your Triumphs: Show off your gaming prowess and experiences on social media platforms with our customizable sharing templates. Let the world know what you're playing and celebrate your achievements with style. Widgets for Quick Access: Access your favorite lists and games right from your home screen with GameLog's convenient widgets. Enjoy instant access to your gaming universe without even opening the app. Seamless Cross-Device Syncing: Experience true gaming continuity as GameLog automatically synchronizes your lists and games across all your devices with iCloud. Enjoy uninterrupted access and effortlessly pick up where you left off. Support: If you find GameLog to be an essential part of your gaming routine, please consider leaving a positive review and supporting Matt, the sole developer behind this app. Unlock the full potential of GameLog with a GameLog Pro subscription, directly contributing to future enhancements and updates. We value your feedback: For any questions, concerns, or feature requests, reach out to us via the support screen in the app's settings tab. We are here to assist you and make your GameLog experience exceptional. Subscription Details: By purchasing a GameLog Pro subscription, your iTunes account will be charged. Auto-renewal is enabled by default, with payment charged within 24 hours prior to the end of the current subscription period. You have the flexibility to manage your subscription and turn off auto-renewal at any time by accessing your Account Settings in the App Store.

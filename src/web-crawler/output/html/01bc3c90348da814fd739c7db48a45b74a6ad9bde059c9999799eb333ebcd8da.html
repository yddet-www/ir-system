What is NFL Sunday Ticket and what games are included?

NFL Sunday Ticket is a premium out-of-market sports package that broadcasts all out-of-market regular season Sunday National Football League games not available on your local broadcast in your area (NBC, CBS or FOX affiliate). NFLST excludes digital only games. NFLST is not available for commercial usage. This means it's intended for private homes and apartments. For example, if you're a Dallas Cowboys fan but live in Miami, you'd be able to watch all of the Sunday Cowboys games that aren't shown on NBC, CBS or FOX in your local market.

What is NFL RedZone?

NFL Sunday Ticket with NFL RedZone included is an option at checkout to both YouTube TV and YouTube users. How do I purchase NFL RedZone with YouTube TV?

NFL RedZone is available for YouTube TV members to purchase. There are three ways for users to do this:

  • If you are signing up for YouTube TV and NFL Sunday Ticket together, your can add the NFL Redzone with Sports Plus add-on for $10.99/month.
  • If you already have YouTube TV and want to add NFL RedZone to your plan, you have the option to purchase the NFL Sunday Ticket with NFL RedZone or the NFL RedZone with Sports Plus add-on.
  • If you already have both YouTube TV and NFL Sunday Ticket (without RedZone), you can add the NFL RedZone with Sports Plus add on for $10.99/month.

Lastly, if you purchased YouTube TV with NFL Sunday Ticket + NFL RedZone last season and are on an auto-renewing plan, you do not need to purchase it again. You can check your YouTube TV Settings to see your membership status.

If I'm a commercial establishment, can I use my NFL Sunday Ticket subscription?

No. NFL Sunday Ticket on YouTube and YouTube TV is exclusively licensed for residential use. This means it's intended for private homes and apartments.

Commercial establishments, such as bars, restaurants, and stores, are not permitted to use NFL Sunday Ticket unless they obtain specific commercial rights. These commercial rights are not offered through YouTube or YouTube TV. They are available through Everpass Media. For more information, please visit: https://everpass.com/live-sports/nfl-sunday-ticket/

Am I eligible for the new user price of $37.50/mo ($150 for the season) for NFL Sunday Ticket?

The new user price is available only if you have never purchased or subscribed to NFL Sunday Ticket in the past via YouTube or YouTube TV. This includes previous subscriptions through a YouTube TV bundle. Eligible new users may select one of two payment methods: a single payment of $150, or 4 non-cancelable monthly payments of $37.50 (payment plans not available everywhere). The initial payment will be charged at the time of purchase.

If you’re a returning user, find more information on available discounts below.

What is the price for returning NFL Sunday Ticket users, and how do I know if I qualify?

If you have previously purchased NFL Sunday Ticket via YouTube or YouTube TV, you qualify as a returning user. The price for returning users for a standalone purchase on YouTube is a one-time payment of $264, or 4 non-cancelable monthly payments of $66. The price for returning users as an add-on to a YouTube TV base plan is a one-time payment of $204, or 4 non-cancelable monthly payments of $51. The first payment is charged at the time of purchase.

What is the Month-to-Month plan?

With a Month-to-Month plan, you commit to paying for one month at a time and can cancel at any point before your next billing date. Your subscription will auto-renew each month during the season and will automatically end once the NFL regular season concludes. Refunds are not offered for this plan.

Family controls for peace of mind

We believe in expanding the positive power of play. Everyone should feel welcome, safe and in control of their gaming experience. That's why the EA app provides parents and guardians with tools to help them manage their kids’ playtime, spending and more.

 

Learn more about

 the EA app

Manage your whole family in one place

With Families on the EA app, you can see and manage how everyone plays - all in one spot. And once you’ve set up your Family, you won’t need to track down everyone's account information each time you want to check in.

Child Accounts

  • Parental verification required
  • No access to online play or social features

Teen Accounts

  • Online chat & play with friends enabled
  • Parent-controlled spending
  • Zero-dollar default spending
  • A weekly Family Activity Report showing play habits

Learn more about

 Families on EA app

Our Tools. Your Rules.

We’ve introduced more ways for you to take charge of how your family plays. On the EA app, parents and guardians can set monthly spending limits and sign up to receive weekly Family Activity Reports that detail your family’s play habits.

Playtime Controls

Manage daily schedules, apply or customize playtime guidelines, and see how much time everyone spends playing.

Spending Controls

Set monthly limits on how much your kids can spend on games and other content.

Stay in the know

Understanding how your family plays should be simple. That’s why we’ll notify you if your teen approaches or exceeds the playtime guideline you set.

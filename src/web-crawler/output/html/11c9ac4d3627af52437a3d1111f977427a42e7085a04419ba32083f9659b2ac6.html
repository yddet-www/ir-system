    DaveyLikesApps

    First of all, I’d like to say that the devs here do genuinely listen to the complaints and fix any problem. Now then, there’re still some issues present, such as: 1️⃣Not enough available platforms, it’d be nice to see platforms like Epic Games, GOG, etc…as well. And associated with the Steam portion, it’d be nice to have Steam Deck compatibility details for each game. 2️⃣When browsing items and I happen to see ones that are already on my wishlist—it will sometimes not present it as such unless you attempt to (re-)add it to your wishlist, in which then the app remembers and tells you it’s already there. 3️⃣Details regarding pricing don’t update in real-time…there’ll be situations where price information contradicts. 4️⃣Finally, there are no notification options for the app, there’s only the standard Email notification option (which does do the job), but I’d like notifications from the app itself—with a counter on the app icon telling me the number of new deals available on my wishlist.

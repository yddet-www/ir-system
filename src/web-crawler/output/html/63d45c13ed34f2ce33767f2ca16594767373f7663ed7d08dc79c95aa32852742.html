Digimon Story Time Stranger - What You Need to Know

October 3, 2025

Available now in Standard, Deluxe, and Ultimate Editions!

Platforms

  • PlayStation®5

  • Steam

Digimon Story Time Stranger is an RPG with monster-taming elements that explores the deep bond between humans and Digimon in an epic story that unravels the mystery of the world’s collapse. Embark on an adventure that spans across the human world and Digital World, collecting and raising a diverse array of Digimon to fight in turn-based battles.

Story


Take on a mission to uncover the mystery of the world’s collapse, where chance encounters with unique characters will shape your journey across time and parallel worlds – and change fate itself.

Gameplay and Features

Obtaining Digimon

Digimon have long been suspected of a causal correlation to anomalies throughout history. Digimon are also our best counter to such threats.

Through battles, you can analyze the enemy Digimon. Once analysis is complete, you can then create that Digimon. This process is known as Convert.

Once the scan rate reaches 100% or higher, you can Convert the data into a new Digimon. However, the closer it is to 200%, the stronger the Digimon will be.

Training Digimon

Setup

Once you have your Digimon, you can equip them with Attachment Skills and equipment.

Attachment Skills are equipped freely and can let Digimon attack with different elements or gain skills that boost certain abilities.

Equipment can be used to increase your Digimon’s various stats and resistances.

Digivolution

Digivolution is non-linear, so you can even De-Digivolve into younger forms to set them on the Digivolution path you need.

Each Digimon must reach certain conditions to Digivolve into other Digimon forms. The stats that grow the most can affect which Digivolutions they’ll be able to reach.

Personality

Each Digimon has a different personality. Depending on their Personality, the way their stats increase when they level up is different.

These changes also affect the Digimon’s Digivolution path.

DigiFarm

Here, Digimon can train and grow on their own. You can boost their stats through training. This will help your Digimon become even stronger. Provide them with food to increase their bond points.

Agent Skill

As an ADAMAS agent, you can also help your Digimon grow with Agent Skills. You can unlock your Digimon’s abilities using Anomaly Points. Complete missions to earn more!

Your bond with your Digimon plays a critical role, so be sure to explore the types of bonds you can build with them.

Play the Demo

It’s time to take your first steps into the World of Digimon. The demo for Digimon Story Time Stranger is available now! Game progress will carry over from the demo, so try it out today!

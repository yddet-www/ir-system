Playsit — community for game lovers. Our database brings together over 115,000 games from various services: Steam, PlayStation, Xbox, Switch, and others. What's available here? — Find out about new games and trailers — Keep track of the boards, create your own and share them with other players — Read, write and comment reviews — Find games and users using convenient filters — Find out detailed information about your favorite game — Write reviews and share them with other players — Organize your own gaming library — Keep in touch with your friends — Compete with others in the leaderboard — Get exclusive features and support Playsit — Find communities PERSONALIZED RECOMMENDATIONS. Rate games, add them to your collection, and receive personalized game recommendations and suggestions. YOUR GAME LIBRARY. Finished Alan Wake 2? Tap "Played" rate, write a review, and share with friends. Waiting for GTA 6? Tap "Will Play" and we'll notify you when the game is released. Create themed boards and save games on them – make them public or private for sharing with other users. NEW RELEASES. Watch fresh trailers and gameplay of your favorite games anytime, anywhere. CHECK PRICES. Check prices for games in stores like PlayStation Store, Xbox Store, and Steam. FRIENDS' ACTIVITY. In the notification feed, you'll find your friends' gaming activity: ratings, recent reviews, and games they're looking forward to. PERSONAL PROFILE. Customize your profile according to your preferences, track games, rate them, follow subscribers, and much more. Don't forget to set a unique status! COMMUNITIES. Create your own communities or find people with similar interests! —————————— Playsit Plus offers exclusive features and supports us: EMOJI STATUSES. Choose emoji to display current activity next to your name. PROFILE BADGE. A badge next to your name showing that you are helping support Playsit. PROFILE COVER. Upload personal covers and make your profile more attractive. UNLIMITED BOARDS. Create an infinite number of boards and save up infinite games. PUBLIC BOARDS. Make your boards public to show them on the main page. Give new life to your game collection and join the exciting world of gaming with Playsit!

  • Great start; need a couple of things

    Jan 18

    nsdonze

    First off, I am so happy we finally have something like this on mobile. I’ve been looking for something like this for a long time, but could only find sites online. Compared to those sites, I think this is a great start, and actually does a lot better than the sites I used in the past. But we NEED two things: the ability to mark a game as played without having to leave a rating, and a “playing” status for games we haven’t finished yet. These two features would make it perfect. Obviously rating is a big part of apps like this, but a bigger priority for me is keeping a log of everything I’ve ever played. In that case, I haven’t played some games since I was a kid; I don’t want to have to leave a rating based on a 20 year old memory. Like I said, this is a great start and I’m excited to start adding. I hope we get these features. Thank you!!

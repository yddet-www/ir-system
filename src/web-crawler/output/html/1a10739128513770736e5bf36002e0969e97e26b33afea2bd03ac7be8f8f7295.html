Long ago, the "Patapons" flourished thanks to the power of their Mighty. However, when Mighty departed, the Patapons gradually declined, and now they live dejectedly in a corner of the world, on the verge of extinction.. 
"Patapon" are mysterious and cute one-eyed creatures. They only obey the rhythm of the drums played by you, their "Mighty".

What is PATAPON 1?

Long ago, the Patapons flourished through the power of their Mighty.
But when the Mighty vanished, the Patapons gradually fell into decline. Now, they live in despair, tucked away in a remote corner of the world on the brink of extinction...

It is to these Patapons that you, the player, appear as a new “Mighty”!

The Patapons have a legend that says, “If you reach Earthend and see IT, you will find happiness.” Yet another tale warns, “If you see IT, your eyes will be burned.”

To uncover the truth behind the legend, you will lead the Patapons on a grand adventure to reach Earthend as their new Mighty!

What is PATAPON 2?

In their previous adventure (Patapon 1), the Patapons realized just how small the world they had known truly was. Driven by curiosity, they set out on a long voyage to explore a wider world.

But on the 49th day of their journey, disaster struck! A giant monster emerged from the sea and attacked, sinking the Patapons’ ship.

Washed ashore in a foreign land, the Patapons found themselves barely clinging to life... It is to these Patapons that you, the player, once again appear as their “Mighty”.

As the Patapons begin rebuilding their tribe, they encounter a mysterious “Patapon wearing a mask who has lost his memory”—the “Hero.”

According to an old saying: “A funny-looking mask dude will save the world after meeting the Mighty…”

And so, your journey begins once more. As their Mighty, you will lead the Patapons on a grand new adventure to save the world!

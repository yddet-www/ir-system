聯繫方式

 

與IARC有關的疑問應當被發送至： info@globalratings.com.

 

與IARC分配的具體分級相關的疑問、意見或投訴應當被發送給相關分級機構：
Contact Information

 

Questions related to IARC should be sent to: info@globalratings.com.

 

If you choose to contact IARC, we will collect whatever personal information you include in your email to us (e.g., email address, name, etc.), which we will use to address your inquiry. Your personal information will be transferred to us in the United States. Protections for personal information may not be as robust in the United States as they are in your home country. By sending us your personal information, you consent to our collection and transfer of it to the United States.

 

Questions, comments or complaints pertaining to specific IARC-assigned ratings should be directed to the appropriate rating authority:
KONTAKT

 

Für Fragen zu IARC wenden Sie sich bitte an: info@globalratings.com.

 

Bei Fragen, Kommentaren oder Beschwerden zu bestimmten IARC-Ratings wenden Sie sich bitte direkt an die entsprechende regional zuständige Institution.
お問い合わせ

 

IARCに関連するご質問は info@globalratings.com までご連絡ください。

 

IARCが割り当てた特定の評価に関するご質問、コメント、苦情は、該当する評価機関にまで直接ご連絡ください。
연락처

 

IARC 관련 질문은 info@globalratings.com 으로 문의하십시오.

 

IARC 등급 평가와 관련된 질문, 의견 또는 불만 사항은 관련 등급 평가 기관에 직접 문의해 주십시오.
CONTATO

 

Perguntas relacionadas à instituição IARC devem ser enviadas para: info@globalratings.com.

 

Perguntas, comentários ou reclamações relativas a classificações específicas atribuídas por meio do sistema de classificação IARC devem ser direcionadas à autoridade de classificação apropriada:
CONTACTO

 

Las preguntas relacionadas con la IARC deben enviarse a: info@globalratings.com.

 

Las preguntas, los comentarios o las quejas relacionadas con calificaciones específicas asignadas por la IARC deben ser dirigidas a la autoridad de calificación correspondiente:
Australian Classification Board - Australia
Classificação Indicativa (ClassInd) - Brazil
Digital Game Self-regulation Committee - Taiwan
Entertainment Software Rating Board (ESRB) - North America
Game Rating and Administration Committee (GRAC) - Republic of Korea
Pan European Game Information (PEGI) - Europe
Unterhaltungssoftware Selbstkontrolle (USK) - Germany

PATAPON

Patapon

A delicate, mysterious species of creatures reminiscent of an eyeball. They embark on a journey to the Earthend, driven by the fable that "True happiness will arrive when you see IT at the Earthend.



MEDEN

Patapon

A revered priestess of the Patapons, Meden is a reliable guide that can be a little goofy at times.



HATAPON

Patapon

Bravely leads the Patapons' charge into battle by waving the flag. If the Hatapon is defeated, the battle is lost even if there are remaining Patapons.



YARIPON

Patapon

These reliable Yaripons attack by throwing their spears!

Yaripons are great for attacking enemies in high places or at a distance with their long-ranged spears.



TATEPON

Patapon

Tatepons offer great defense and offense with their trusty shield-and-sword combo!

Tatepons have the highest defense among all Patapons and are essential in battles against strong enemies.



YUMIPON

Patapon

The feisty Yumipons attack from a distance by firing their sharp arrows!

While Yumipons offer great offense for long-ranged enemies, they're at the mercy of the wind which could blow their aim off course.



KIBAPON

Patapon

Kibapons ride into battle upon horseback and attack with their lances! Due to their speed, they can cause immediate havoc deep in enemy territory.

While great in battle, Kibapons aren't ideal for hunting because the smell of their horses cause prey to flee early.



DEKAPON

Patapon

Dekapons are solid offensive units, attacking with their hammers and clubs. What they lack in speed, they make up for with their high attack power.



MEGAPON

Patapon

Megapons attack by firing unique sound missiles from their horns. These missiles have a piercing effect, making them reliable in critical moments.



ROBOPON

Patapon

Characterized by their robotic arms, Robopons are powerful melee units that deal relatively high damage and good at destroying enemy buildings and obstacles.


TORIPON

Patapon

The high-flying Toripons ride birds into battle raining javelins onto enemies. Their aerial attacks allow them to dodge many attacks.

They take advantage of their mobility to stay out of harm’s way, but don’t let them get grounded by freezing or sleep effects!


MAHOPON

Patapon

Mahopons are versatile magic users, with their attacks changing based on the staff they wield. They're the only Patapons capable of healing.


HERO

Patapon

The Hero is a masked Patapon who lost their memories. They can be customized to fit any role by changing their mask and subsequently, their abilities and appearance.


Zigoton

Enemy

The Zigotons are a hostile tribe of Patapon with distinctive square red eyes. They stand in opposition to the Patapon, believing that their journey to see "IT" will bring about a great disaster.

Tips: Expect fierce resistance from Zigotons - adapt your stategy to counter their aggressive tactics!



Gong

Enemy

Gong the Hawkeye is the formidable captain of the Zigoton army, recognizable by his uniquely shaped helm. His immense strength allows him to unleash devastating scythe attacks and summon powerful tornadoes.

Tips: Exploit the openings between Gong's powerful, large-scale attacks by staying mobile and attacking strategically.



KHARMA

Enemy

Queen Kharma is the ruler of the Zigotons, distinguished by her elegant butterfly-like wings. Mysterious and commanding, she plays a crucial role in the conflict against the Patapon.

This mighty Zigoton Queen takes to the skies by flapping her wings!


Hoshipon

Enemy

Hoshipon is a mysterious Patapon with a star-like appearance. Those who take the time to listen to its stories may uncover valuable secrets and rewards.

Tip: Pay close attention to Hoshipon’s words—you never know what useful treasure or knowledge it might reveal!



Karmen

Enemy

The Karmen are a hostile tribe of Patapon, distinguished by their leaf-shaped masks.They stand in direct opposition to the Patapon, eager to retaliate for any encroachment.

Tip: Even the Karmen tribe has a variety of fighting styles! Combine close-range and long-range attacks to disrupt their formation and create openings!


Ormen Karmen

Enemy

Ormen Karmen is the eccentric boss of the Karmen tribe, known for his long arms and ability to fly.

Tip: Ormen's flying ability is very troublesome! When in trouble, long-range attacks are essential!


Demon

Enemy

The Underworld tribe is a mysterious group known for ending their words with "ma."

Tip: Some of them are huge and can attack powerfully, so be careful!


Black Hoshipon

Enemy

Black Hoshipon is a Patapon with a dark, star-like appearance. Beneath its seemingly innocent exterior, something evil lurks, waiting to be unleashed.

Tip: Be careful when you come into contact with Black Hoshipon! Sometimes it can cause major changes in the middle of battle!


DODONGA

Boss

Dodonga is a massive, dinosaur-like beast that uses its powerful jaws and fiery breath to devastate anything in its path. Its sheer size and strength make it a formidable foe in battle.

Tip: Watch Dodonga’s movements carefully and attack after it breathes fire or lunges to exploit its moment of vulnerability!



ZAKNEL

Boss

Zaknel is a colossal caterpillar-like beast that uses fire breath, crushing body slams, and ground-shaking vibrations to paralyze and overwhelm its foes. Its unpredictable attacks make it a dangerous challenge.

Tip：Stay mobile and time your attacks—dodge its tremors and strike in the brief windows between Zaknel's powerful attacks!



GAEEN

Boss

Gaeen is a towering, robot-like colossus that wields immense power, attacking with devastating energy beams and powerful arm smashes. Its mechanical might makes it a relentless force on the battlefield.

Tip：Strike when it leaves an opening between its heavy arm attacks! And be wary of its beams!



CIOKINA

Boss

Ciokina is a massive, crab-like beast that attacks with powerful pincers, hurling them from a distance, and releasing bubbles that can put foes to sleep. Its mix of ranged and status-inflicting attacks makes it a tricky opponent.

Be wary of the sleep bubbles! Seize the opportunity to attack while Ciokina is recovering from its powerful special moves!



SHOOKLE

Boss

Shookle is a monstrous, carnivorous plant that lashes out with whip-like vines and releases a sleep-inducing breath to incapacitate its foes. Its long reach and status effects make it a dangerous adversary.

Tip: Keep your distance to avoid its vines and sleep breath—use ranged attacks to whittle it down safely!



MOCHICHICHI

Boss

Mochichichi is a colossal Motiti that uses its massive beak, crushing hip attacks, and even a sleep-inducing fart to subdue enemies. Its size and unpredictable moves make it a tricky opponent.

Keep your distance from Mochichichi's hip attack and beak attacks! Also, adapt your commands to its various attacks.


MANBOTH

Boss

Manboth is a massive mammoth-like beast that charges, stomps, and unleashes icy breath to freeze its foes. However, its own blizzards can sometimes backfire, freezing Manboth itself.

Tip: Take advantage of moments when Manboth freezes itself—use that opening to unleash powerful attacks!


CENTURA

Boss

Centura is a towering spider-like monster with long legs, attacking by whipping its body and piercing foes with its sharp needle. However, it can only be damaged when it’s raining.

Tip: Time your battle wisely—wait for rain before engaging Centura, and strike hard while the weather is in your favor!


CANOGIAS

Boss

Kanogias is a massive, fortress-like mechanical beast armed with swinging iron balls, powerful cannon fire, and delayed-explosion bombs. Its heavy artillery makes it a formidable opponent.

Tip: Stay on the move to dodge cannon fire and time your attacks between its bomb explosions for a safer assault!


PHARAMATARA

Boss

Pharamatara is a mighty sky guardian that lurks in the clouds, attacking with freezing cloud rings, a powerful bite, and massive hands that can send Patapons flying. Its high mobility makes it a challenging foe.

Tip: Avoid its freezing rings and strike quickly when it lowers itself—timing is key to bringing this sky beast down!

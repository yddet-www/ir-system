Protagonist

The Revenant Hunter, Destined to Save the World

As a Revenant Hunter, the player must prevent the worlds inevitable collapse by journeying 100 years into the past alongside Lou, a mysterious Revenant girl with the power to manipulate time. The player as the protagonist can freely customize their appearances and more in-game.

Lou MagMell

The Protagonist's Counterpart, with the Power to Travel Through Time

A Revenant girl who revived the protagonistwho lost his life during a mission as a Revenant Hunterby giving him her own heart. She holds the special power to follow someone's Pathos and leap into the past. With the mission to save the world, she embarks on a journey to the past alongside the protagonist.

Valentin Voda

The Serene Pioneer of the Progenitor's Bloodline

He is the heir to the Voda House, a Revenant lineage descended from the Progenitor and one of the most formidable bloodlines among their kind. He once led MagMell as a magical research institute, delving into the secrets of ancient formae. He also holds the title of a judicial magistrate, acting as an arbiter to settle disputes between vampires and humans.

Noah G. MagMell

The Warrior of Resolve, Protector of Friends

A former human-turned-Revenant who belonged to Mag Mell's Research Institute 100 years in the past. To protect his friend Valentin, who values his strength above all else, he has become a personal bodyguard, taking on dangerous missions without hesitation.

Jose Anjou

The Tragic Queen Burdened by Guilt

The Revenant who rules the Sunken City. She built shelters to protect the last survivors in the world, but she is also haunted by a failure from her pastan inability to stop a massive Resurgence outbreak that caused unimaginable destruction. With the weight of that guilt, she fights tirelessly on the front lines, a constant guardian for the living.

Lyle McLeish

The Lone Swordsman of the Pledge

A Revenant with exceptional talent, lauded as a master swordsman. He serves as the ace of The Dawn Chorus, an organization fighting to reclaim the Corroded Scar. His reckless fighting style, which disregards his own safety, is feared even by his allies, and he always operates on his own.

Holly Asturias

A Healer's Genius Against a Cursed Disease

A Revenant doctor at a clinic nestled deep in the forest. She wields the healing powers of the Luxuria Bloodline, a lineage rumored to be cursed, to tirelessly research the Resurgence and treat the twisted Revenants it creates.

Lavinia Voda

Heir to a Savior's Mission

A Revenant descendant of the Progenitor who saved the world from collapse. In this decaying world on the verge of ruin, she leads "MagMell", an organization fighting for humanity's salvation. She bestows the mission of salvation upon the protagonist and Lou, sending them back in time.

Lycoris

The Mysterious Girl Named After the Flower of the Dead

 A mysterious girl who appears before Lou and the others in the Undead Forest in the current world. She seems to be someone who knows a great deal about what happened to Holly...
